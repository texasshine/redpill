The Warning Signs

When Protection Becomes a Liability

PPF has a lifespan. In the Texas sun, that lifespan is often shorter than the warranty suggests. If you're seeing these signs, the film is already failing.

Yellowing & Staining

UV exposure and environmental contaminants have permeated the top coat. It's no longer clear; it's an eyesore.

Cracking & Flaking

The film has become brittle. It no longer peels in sheets; it flakes off in tiny pieces, making removal a surgical process.

Straight Talk

We'll Be Honest With You

Older film can bond aggressively to paint — especially on vehicles with prior paintwork or film well past its lifespan. We use the safest methods available. But paint lift is a real risk.

Risk increases when:

  • Film has exceeded its designed lifespan
  • The vehicle has prior body or paint work
  • Paint adhesion was compromised before installation

Every removal starts with an inspection. Every job requires a signed waiver. We don't make promises about outcomes we can't guarantee.
