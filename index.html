Red Pill: Reality Reset

Remove what's failing. Correct the paint. Return to a known baseline.

Red Pill is for you if:

  • You have yellowing or failing PPF
  • Old ceramic coatings that stopped performing
  • Dealer coatings that never worked
  • Layered products that only added confusion

This path focuses on removing layers that are no longer serving the paint.

After removal, the paint is corrected and brought back to baseline. From there, we choose protection based on how you actually drive—not hype or upselling.

Protection may include:

  • Traditional sealants
  • Ceramic coatings
  • Strategic film placement
  • Or simply proper maintenance
Sometimes the right move isn't adding another layer. Sometimes it's removing the ones that were never helping.

Red Pill is about clarity, honesty, and restoration of reality.
